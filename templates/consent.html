This Human Intelligence Task (HIT) is part of a research project by the University of Copenhagen.

This Transcription Task is made up of 4 paragraphs, with every paragraph consisting of 336 characters. You will be asked to confirm current bonus payments, make a typewritten copy of given texts and finally complete a short follow-up survey of 10 questions. This task will pay a basepay of $0.01, bonus payments ranging from $0.05 to $0.15 for every written paragraph and a $0.15 bonus for completing the short follow-up survey. This task will take approximately 15 minutes to complete.

You will only receive payment after successfully submitting this HIT to Amazon Mechanical Turk! Please be aware that by quitting the experiment (e.g. closing the browser window) you will loose your claim for any payment!

All user specific information will be anonymized. No personal information will be stored. You can only complete this HIT once. For further questions please contact us: ucph.mturk@gmail.com
